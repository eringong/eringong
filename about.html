background

    I graduated with a Master's degree from University of Michigan School of Information with a focus in community informatics and evaluation. My first official museum job was as Evaulation and Grants Manager at the Ann Arbor Hands-On Museum in 2008. Before that, I had worked in program development, writing, and marketing.

    In 2010, I transitioned to independent consulting for museums and libraries, primarily in outcomes-based evaluation, strategic planning, and grant writing. I currently live in and work out of the San Francisco Bay Area.

recent clients + projects

  • Troy Public Library. Troy, Michigan. Strategic planning facilitator.

  • Chelsea District Library. Chelsea, Michigan. Strategic planning and evaluation consultant.

  • Ann Arbor Hands-On Museum. Ann Arbor, Michigan. Grant manager for IMLS National Leadership Planning Grant and evaluation consultant.

  • American Museum of Magic. Marshall, Michigan. Program evaluation.

  • R&L Consulting. Arden, North Carolina. Assisted in grant writing and program development for museums.
